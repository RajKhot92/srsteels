WELCOME TO S.R.STEELS

Our History

A vision to bring quality engineering products and machine components became reality in 1982 when two technocrats decided to bring their heads and brains together and started S R Steels. We started as pattern making process unit and gradually we gave it a shape by widening our product range that is backed by superior technical expertise.

What We Do

Our company received the ISO 9001:2015 certification and soon we climbed the leadership ladder. We currently position ourselves as someone who has established oneself as a leading manufacturer of Industrial Valves, Spares and Accessories. We have the well-oiled robust expertise in and requisite facilities to compete with Indian and Global standards that are ingrained in our range of valves manufactured by us.

Our in-house team of engineers ensures that the entire process of production right from drawings, designing pattern, melting metal, moulding and framing the end product --- VALVE goes smoothly and efficiently and take utmost care in preserving its quality.

We Manufacture Extensive Range Of Slurry Valves

Our products

The Valves manufactured by us, are designed to move slurry fluids face numerous challenges. These fluids are thick, filled with solids and quite often highly abrasive material. However these Valves stand the test of time. We won’t deny that before reaching the ultimate product it undergoes several checks and tests where we do find pores. These are then filled and rectified and retested until it achieves desired result.
